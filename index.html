How do I play Sudoku?

Fill the 9x9 grid so that every row, column, and 3x3 box contains the digits 1-9 exactly once. Use logic and deduction to solve the puzzle without guessing.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Zen Number Puzzle works completely offline. No internet connection required to enjoy your puzzles.

How do I change the difficulty?

From the main menu, select your preferred difficulty level: Easy, Medium, Hard, or Expert. Each level offers a different challenge.

Is my progress saved automatically?

Yes, the app automatically saves your progress. You can return to your game anytime without losing your work.

üí° Tips for Better Gameplay

  • Start with the easiest numbers - look for rows, columns, or boxes that are almost complete
  • Use the elimination technique - if a number can only go in one place, put it there
  • Take breaks if you get stuck - a fresh perspective often helps
  • Don't guess - every puzzle has a logical solution
  • Practice regularly to improve your pattern recognition
